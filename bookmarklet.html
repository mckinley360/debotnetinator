Debotnet-inator Bookmarklet Edition

The amazing UltraBear has ported the Debotnet-inator to a bookmarklet so you can use it anywhere on the web!

You can get it by dragging the link below to your bookmarks bar.

Debotnet-inator

If you want to look at the decoded and un-minified script, you can find it here or on the GitHub repository.

Usage

  1. Copy the link you want to debotnet and click your new bookmark
  2. Paste the link in at the prompt
  3. Type your mode in, using the characters outside of the parentheses
  4. The next prompts will ask you about optional parameters. Click 'OK' for yes, and 'Cancel' for no.
  5. The last prompt should show you your debotnetted link or an error. You can copy the link straight from here and paste it anywhere you want.

Enjoy your reduced tracking!
